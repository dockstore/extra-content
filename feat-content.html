EDGE COVID-19 Collection

A collection of standardized workflows from the Los Alamos National Laboratory for genome reference-based assembly and preliminary analysis of Illumina or Nanopore data for SARS-CoV-2 genome sequencing projects based on the fully open source bioinformatics platform, EDGE Bioinformatics software.

SARS-COV-2 IWC Galaxy Workflows

A collection of best-practices Galaxy workflows for read mapping, variant calling and other analyses of Illumina data for SARS-CoV-2 sequencing projects maintained by the Intergalactic Workflow Commission (IWC). IWC maintains high quality galaxy workflows that are reviewed and tested before publication and with every new Galaxy release.

New to containers and workflow languages?

A Human Pangenome researcher discusses the motivation behind using these tools in your research and provides a great introduction to Docker and the Workflow Description Language (WDL).
